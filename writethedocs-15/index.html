Threshold: when is it good enough?

Outcomes

  • Enabling environment
  • Capacities strenghtened
  • Alternatives developed
  • Agendas set
  • Policies changed
  • Practice changed

Don't try to get them all at once! And remember, output DOES NOT EQUAL outcomes. Outcomes are the key ones, then output comes naturally.
